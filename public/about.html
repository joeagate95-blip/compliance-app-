Why We Built Landlord Compliance Hub

For over 10 years I have operated my own business within the gas industry, working closely with landlords, letting agents and property managers across the rental sector.

Throughout that time I repeatedly saw the same problems: documents stored in different locations, certificates expiring unexpectedly, contractor details being lost and jobs being forgotten because of poor communication.

Landlord Compliance Hub was built to solve those problems. Whether you own a single rental property or manage a large portfolio, the platform gives you one place to manage your compliance.

Store documents. Track expiry dates. Manage contractors. Stay organised and never miss a compliance deadline again.

See The Platform In Action

Dashboard Overview

Instantly see the status of your portfolio from one simple dashboard. Track property numbers, uploaded certificates, upcoming expiry dates and expired documents at a glance.

No more searching through folders, emails or spreadsheets to find the information you need.

Property Compliance Centre

Each property has its own dedicated compliance page. Quickly view Gas Safety Certificates, EICRs, EPCs, PAT Testing records, Legionella assessments, Fire Safety documents and Tenant Contracts.

Everything is organised in one location and available whenever you need it.

Making Communication Easier

One of the biggest frustrations for landlords is organising contractors and keeping track of ongoing work.

Jobs can easily be delayed because messages are missed, emails get buried or nobody is quite sure whether the contractor has actually booked the work.

The Contractor Centre was designed to simplify this process. Request work, monitor progress, upload certificates and track completion without endless emails and phone calls.

Ever found a brilliant contractor and then been unable to find their number six months later? Store all your trusted contractors in one place and access them whenever you need them.
